We at the Emerald Leaf strive to serve you the most vibrant, flamboyant and importantly authentic Indian food in an opulent atmosphere. Our essential key to ensuring our dishes ooze the spirit of India in every bite are our ingredients.

Only organically cultivated stock is used by our vigorously trained in-house chefs. Our vendors are an exclusively selected group of farmers who grow their vegetables and fruits in their farms and then bring them to the floating market of Srinagar to sell. The Emerald Leaf is the perfect blend grandeur and fine culinary dining. We invite you to this life lasting experience.
